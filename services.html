Education

Education is one of the most concerned areas in MOTHER charitable organization. We strongly believe the right for education among children and our love and service has specially moved us towards education and socialization of children and adults with special needs.

Developing skills of children and adults with special needs is empowering them with education, skills, attitudes and values to show the world what they are capable of. MOTHER foundation has established two centers for them.

Pathum uyana center for children and adults with special needs in Aluthgama was opened on the 16th of February, 2011. Classes are held on every week day. At present 25 children and adults, between the ages of 5 - 30, are studying with the support of four staff members.

Niduk Piyasa center for children and adults with special needs was opened on the 13th of October, 2014. Classes are held on every week day. At present 20 children and adults, between the ages of 5 – 30, are studying with the support of four staff members.

In our centers we train them and provide them with day life skills, Academic training, esthetic skills (music therapy, drama therapy and art therapy), behavioral therapy, social skills, vocational skills, physiotherapy, and multi sensory room facilities.

Future Projects

“MOTHER” Home of Love is our new project to serve the disadvantaged people in the society especially to those people who are rejected and discriminated by the society. They are all welcome to our home of love. It can be AIDS patients, cancer patients, people who are ill and rejected from the society, who are not accepted to their own homes, people who are rejected by friends, families, relatives etc. its open to anyone who needs our love and care.

They will live in a collaborative and non judgmental society within the home and they can even carry out their normal life patterns.

They can work, go to their jobs, continue their education and have a normal life in friendly surroundings in our home of love.

Our main purpose of building up the home of love is to give them the self – recognition, self –worth and help them to live a normal life.

Let’s make them feel that they belong to the society and they also can live a life full of hope and joy.
