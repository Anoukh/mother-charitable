Volunteers

We warmly welcome volunteers within Sri Lanka and from other countries to join us and experience the joy of serving another.

Give a bit from your precious time to disadvantaged children and adults.

Persons with good health conditions are welcome to serve with us people who are disabled also welcome but they will face some limitation than the others still for all we welcome all of you.

It’s not about profession, wealth, power, education level or status it’s all about our ability to serve people through love.

You have to be an adoptable, patience and a kind hearted person to become our volunteer that’s the only qualities we are concern about.

If you are interested to spend time with disadvantaged children and adults, serve them trough your knowledge and your skills and become a volunteer in MOTHER charitable foundation please contact us.
