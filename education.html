PathumUyana

Pathum uyana is a center of Education and Skill Development for Children and Adults with special needs, Aluthgama, Sri Lanka

PathumUyana is located in the Beruwala provincial council, belonging to Kalutara district. There is a population of 24831 who are with special needs within the kalutara district. The breakdown of them is as follows.

  • Visual impairments - 4690
  • Hearing impairments - 5043
  • Physical disability - 9581
  • Intellectual impairment - 4809
  • Disability - 808


Source : -
Census of population and houses 2001
Census and Statistic Department


Within the Beruwala provincial council, live almost 1000 people with special needs. Kalutara district has 3 schools for them where 200 children are currently studying.

Pathum Uyana center was opened on the 16th of February, 2011

Classes are held from 9.00am to 2.00pm on every week day. At present 25 children and adults, between the ages of 5 – 30, are studying with the support of four staff members.

NidukPiyasa

Niduk Piyasa is a center of Education and Skill Development for Children and Adults with special needs in Kirinda, Mathara, Sri Lanka

Niduk Piyasa is located in Kirinda Divisional Secretariat, belonging to Mathara district. There is a population of 15255 who are with special needs within the Mathara district. The breakdown of them is as follows.

  • Visual impairments – 3773
  • Hearing impairments – 4320
  • Physical disability – 7299
  • Intellectual impairment – 4379
  • Other disability – 681

Almost 1000 people live within Kirinda provincial council with special needs. Mathara district has 2 schools for children with special needs, where 300 children are currently studying.

Niduk Piyasa center was opened on the 13th of October, 2014

Classes are held from 8.30am to 1.00pm on every week day. At present 20 children and adults, between the ages of 5 – 30, are studying with the support of four staff members.


Socializing process

Children and adults with special needs are always provided with skills and attitudes they lack in socializing process in our centers. Group works, behavioral therapies, drama therapies’ are conducted in order to enable them to develop self confidence.

Children are directed to many functions such as dancing events, sport meets, cultural shows, talent shows, Christmas carols, New Year festivals etc and they are encouraged to participate in these evens so that they learn to move with the society, identify their skills and talents, learn coping skills, learn social interaction and feel normal in the society.


Behavior modifications

We pay a special attention on behavior modification of children and adults with special needs. Mostly the impairment of speech, inappropriate behaviors, lack of expressiveness, and lack of interactions are identified and developed individually. Each person gets a unique attention and care from our specially trained staff to develop appropriate behavior and to reduce inappropriate behaviors in their lives.


Physical development

Physical disability lies as a barrier preventing there development. To strengthen physical abilities of adults and children with special needs, we provide physiotherapy and multi sensory room facilities in our centers. Persons who are in need of these facilities are recognized accordingly and treated with care towards a long term physical development.


Livelihood development

Vocational training

We provide vocational training for children and adults with special needs according to their skills and capacity, and doing so we expect to build up their independency, self-confidence and provide economic relief to them and to their families.

We train them along with a guardian who can help the person in future challenges they face in their occupation.

We also get involved in providing them with initial cost and finding suitable market for their products.

Our special attention is to develop them to a capacity that enables them to manage their own start a self-employment.
